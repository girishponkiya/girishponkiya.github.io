About Me

I am a Data Scientist (Assistant VP) at Institutional Shareholder Services (ISS STOXX), where I leverage Natural Language Processing (NLP) to enhance financial data collection and monitor ESG controversies. I hold a Ph.D. in NLP from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay).

My passion lies in the intersection of research and real-world application. I am deeply interested in data structures, algorithms, machine learning, and formal verification, constantly seeking ways to push the boundaries of language processing technologies.
